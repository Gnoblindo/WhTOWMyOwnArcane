Grimgor's 'Ardboyz

Army of Infamy

Characters

Up to 50% of your army may be spent on:
- 1 Black Orc Warboss or Grimgor Ironhide
- 0-1 Black Orc Warboss per 1000 pts
- 0-1 Black Orc Big Bosses or Orc Warbosses per 1000 pts
- Orc Big Bosses, Goblin Bosses
- 0-1 Orc Weirdnob and Orc Weirdboy

Cores

At least 33% of your army must be spent on:
- 0-1 Black Orc Mobs per Black Orc Warboss(at least 1)
- Orc Mobs
- 0-1 Goblin Mob per Goblin Boss

Specials

Up to 50% of your army may be spent on:
- Black Orc Mobs
- 0-1 Orc Boar Boyz per Orc Warboss
- 0-1 Da Immortulz

Rares

Up to 25% of your army may be spent on:
- Giants
- 0-1 Black Orc or Orc Boar Chariots per 1000 pts

Mercenaries

Up to 25% of your army may be spent on:
- Badland Ogre Bulls
- Bonegrinder Giants

Battle Standard Bearer

A single Black Orc Big Boss in your army may be upgraded to be your Battle Standard Bearer for +30 pts. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
If you don't have a unit of "Da Immortulz", then your Battle Standard Bearer must use "Blasted Banner"(see Da Immortulz) for 55 pts.

Magic Items

Any model within this army that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase magic items from the Common or Orc & Goblin Tribes magic item list.



































Grimgor's 'Ardboyz Special Rules

Immortulz

A unit with this rule gives a +1 CR modyfier in Combat and after each Combat phase it regains D3 lost models to a maximum number of half(round to bottom) of models lost in that phase. Both bonuses are lost after it loses Combat Result. A character with this rule regains one wound after overkilling a character in challange by at least 2 wounds.

Marching 'Ardboyz

All Black Orc Mobs have this rule. Orc Mobs can purchase it for +1 pts per model.
This is a special formation. It have ranks like Close and Open Order. Units in that formation can always march, but can move maximum 6" each turn. For it Difficult Terrain counts as Dangerous and Dangerous as Impossible. Gives +1 to armour of models in it. Can't have Marching Column and changes to Close Order when declaring a charge.

Grimgor's Bestests

All Orc & Goblin units in this army have +D3 modyfier to their charge roll and +3 to maximum charge range.

Bodyguard

While your Black Orc Warboss, who is also your General is within 3" of this unit and is hit with a Ranged Attack,
roll a D6. On 2+ this unit is hit instead. Also they count as Black Orc Mob for Da Boyz rule. All of them can issue and accept challanges.

Blasted Banner

This unit gets Magic Resistance(-1) and Unbreakable, but its unbreakable is changed to Stubborn after it used it.
