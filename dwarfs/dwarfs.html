Dwarfs

Army of Infamy

Characters

Up to 50% of your army may be spent on:
- 1 Dwarf Lord or High King
- 0-1 Dwarf Lord per 1000 pts
- 0-1 Dwarf Thanes or Dwarf Lords per 1000 pts
- Dwarf Thanes, Dwarf Engineers
- 0-1 Runesmith and Runesmith Lord

Cores

At least 33% of your army must be spent on:
- 0-1 Dwarf Warriors per Dwarf Lord(at least 1)
- Dwarf Warriors
- 0-1 Dwarf Miners per Dwarf Engineer

Specials

Up to 50% of your army may be spent on:
- Dwarf Warriors
- 0-1 Dwarf Hammerers per Dwarf Lord
- 0-1 Ironbreakers

Rares

Up to 25% of your army may be spent on:
- Giants
- 0-1 Dwarf or Dwarf Chariots per 1000 pts

Mercenaries

Up to 25% of your army may be spent on:
- Ogre Bulls
- Bonegrinder Giants

Battle Standard Bearer

A single Dwarf Thane in your army may be upgraded to be your Battle Standard Bearer for +30 pts. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
If you don't have a unit of "Ironbreakers", then your Battle Standard Bearer must use "Blasted Banner"(see Ironbreakers) for 55 pts.

Magic Items

Any model within this army that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase magic items from the Common or Dwarf Tribes magic item list.



































Dwarfs Special Rules

Ironbreakers

A unit with this rule gives a +1 CR modifier in Combat and after each Combat phase it regains D3 lost models to a maximum number of half(round to bottom) of models lost in that phase. Both bonuses are lost after it loses Combat Result. A character with this rule regains one wound after overkilling a character in challenge by at least 2 wounds.

Marching Dwarfs

All Dwarf Warriors have this rule. Dwarf Warriors can purchase it for +1 pts per model.
This is a special formation. It has ranks like Close and Open Order. Units in that formation can always march, but can move maximum 6" each turn. For it Difficult Terrain counts as Dangerous and Dangerous as Impossible. Gives +1 to armour of models in it. Can't have Marching Column and changes to Close Order when declaring a charge.

Best of the Best

All Dwarf units in this army have +D3 modifier to their charge roll and +3 to maximum charge range.
