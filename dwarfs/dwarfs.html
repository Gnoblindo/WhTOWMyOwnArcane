Clan Angrund Special Rules

Oath of Karak Eight Peaks

When units in this army Defending an Obstacle, models in that unit have Unbreakable and hatred(everything) special rule.

Vengeful Ancestors

At the beginning of the game, choose one enemy Hero per 1000 pts. When it is killed score additional 50 VP and when it kills your Hero, roll a D6. On 4+ place a Royal Spectre of type of the killed character, with equipment depending on its type, but without Runes.

Dwarfen Determination

Your army may re-roll failed Break and Panic tests when in Command Range of a King.

Grudge to Settle

When enemy captures your banner, all your units get +1 to hit against that unit. When you destroy that unit, your enemy loses your banner.

Zhufbar Battleline Special Rules

Crewman Pride

Your War Machines gain Immune to Psychology, Unbreakable and Hatered(everything) special rules. Your Engineers within 3" of them also gain these rules, and when 1 of them is being Charged, all War Machines and Engineers in 6" may move into that combat.

Solid Hammer Wall

All Warriors, Longbeards and Royal Clan Warriors in this army may buy Stubborn and Immune to Psychology for +2 pts/model.

Zhufbar's Finest

0-1 War Machine in Special category may be upgraded to Zhufbar's Finest for +150 pts. They gain +2 Wounds on both crew and machine, Base Size of 100 x 150mm, Large Target special rule, limit to only 1 rune, 5+ Armour Save and special Weapon upgrade:
  • Cannon: Now not only targets on the line of Fire are hit, but you Swipe the 3" template with that line. All under it risk being hit. Those attacks have 10Strength, no Armour Saves allowed and deal Multiple Wounds(D3+1). Now it has Range of 200" and moves through Terrain and Monsters.
  • Bolt Thrower: Improves range to 68". Now no Armour or Regeneration Saves are allowed against this weapon and with each shot you roll an Artillery Die. Its result adds that much Strength to a normal 4 of this weapon. It now ignores cover, long range, gains +1 to Hit and deals Multiple Wounds(D3 +2).
  • Stone Thrower: Now shoots 5" template, Deals Multiple Wounds(D6 +1) on direct Hit, Has Strength 5(10) and no Armour Saves are allowed against it(on center. Outside it normal AP applies). It scatters by 2" less always.
Note that: Unfortunately Zhufbar's Finest War Machines have also Risks. The first Artillery Die they roll each shot Shakes the Machine. If Missfire was rolled, that machine is immediately removed from play, destroying also other Machines in 6". Other numbers just Shake. You may not Shoot again if Shaken. Each Command Phase you look at the number that was rolled, and on the Crew number of this Machine. If the Shake number was Higher, you may not shoot this turn, and lose 1 Crew Wound. You then remove the Shake.

New Dwarven Runes


Hull Runes

These Hull Runes may be enscribed on any type of Dwarven Aircraft. Points cap of those runes depends on type of that Aircraft.
  • Battle Barge - 100pts
  • Gyrobomber - 50pts
  • Gyrocopters or Scout Gyrocopters - 30pts
In units such as Gyrocopters, it counts as single magic item, affecting all models in this unit.



Master Rune of Bombardment - 40pts

Battle Barge or Gyrobomber with this rune gains +1 modifier to Wound roll when using Bombarding Cataclysm or Bombing Run special rule.


Rune of Propulsion - 15pts

This unit gains +1 to its Fly special rule(stackable to max 10).


Rune of Aerial Precision - 35pts

This unit gains +1 to its Bs characteristic.


Master Rune of Honour - 30pts

It is a Hull Rune, but may also be given for an Engineer. On an Aircraft: Single model in this unit may become a Battle Standard Bearer for +15pts.

On an Engineer: This Engineer gains an option to mount an Aircraft. Points of this Aircraft are added to cost of this Engineer. It counts as riding a Behemoth, while using Split Profile(Chariot). Rune of Plating - 20pts

This unit gains Armoured Hide(1) special rule against shooting(Stackable up to 2).


Rune of Arrival - 10pts

This unit may re-roll its first Charge roll in the game.


Master Rune of Air Ambush - 25pts

This unit may be placed in Ambush, but when placing it, you may set it on map as if it was Scout before start of game. It will be unable to move this turn if it used this special type of deployment.
