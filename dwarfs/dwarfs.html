Clan Angrund

Army of Infamy

Characters

Up to 50% of your army may be spent on:
- 0-2 Royal Clan Spectres per 1000 pts
- 1 King
- 0-1 Anvil of Doom, Runelord or King per 2000 pts
- Thanes and Engineers

Cores

At least 25% of your army must be spent on:
- Warriors or Royal Clan Warriors
- 0-1 Thunderers or Longbeards per 1000 pts
- 1 Oath Warriors per Royal Spectre

Specials

Up to 50% of your army may be spent on:
- Longbeards or Thunderers
- 0-1 Cannons per 1000 pts
- 0-2 Grudge Throwers or Bolt Throwers per 1000 pts

Rares

Up to 25% of your army may be spent on:
- 0-1 Flame Cannon or Organ Gun per 1000 pts
- 0-1 Rangers

Mercenaries

Up to 25% of your army may be spent on:
- Imperial Dwarves
- 0-1 Doomseekers per 1000 pts

Battle Standard Bearer

A single Thane in your army may be upgraded to be your Battle Standard Bearer for +25 pts. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on runic items a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single runic standard for up to 100 pts.

Magic Items

Any model within this army that have the option to purchase runic items may purchase runic items from the Dwarfen Mountain Holds rune list.



































Clan Angrund Special Rules

Oath of Karak Eight Peaks

When units in this army Defending an Obstacle, models in thet unit have Unbreakable and hatered(everything) special rule.

Vengeful Ancestors

At the beggining of the game, choose one enemy character per 1000 pts. When it is killed score additional 200 VP and when it kills your character, roll a D6. On 4+ place a Royal Spectre of type of the killed character.

Dwarfen Determination

Your army may re-roll failed Break and Panic tests when in Command Range of a King.

Grudge to Settle

When enemy captures your banner, all your units get +1 to hit against that unit. When you destroy that unit, your enemy looses your banner.
