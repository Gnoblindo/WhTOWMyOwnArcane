Clan Angrund

Army of Infamy

Characters

Up to 50% of your army may be spent on:
- 0-2 Royal Clan Spectres per 1000 pts
- 1 King
- 0-1 Anvil of Doom, Runelord or King per 1000 pts
- Thanes and Engineers

Cores

At least 25% of your army must be spent on:
- Warriors
- 0-1 Thunderers or Longbeards per 1000 pts
- 1 Oath Warriors per Royal Spectre

Specials

Up to 50% of your army may be spent on:
- Longbeards or Thunderers
- 0-1 Cannons per 1000 pts
- 0-2 Grudge Throwers or Bolt Throwers per 1000 pts

Rares

Up to 25% of your army may be spent on:
- 0-1 Flame Cannon or Organ Gun per 1000 pts
- 0-1 Rangers

Mercenaries

Up to 25% of your army may be spent on:
- Imperial Dwarves
- 0-1 Doomseekers per 1000 pts

Battle Standard Bearer

A single Thane in your army may be upgraded to be your Battle Standard Bearer for +25 pts. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on runic items a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase Standard Runes with no point limit.

Magic Items

Any model within this army that have the option to purchase runic items may purchase runic items from the Dwarfen Mountain Holds rune list.



























Clan Angrund Special Rules

Oath of Karak Eight Peaks

When units in this army Defending an Obstacle, models in thet unit have Unbreakable and hatered(everything) special rule.

Vengeful Ancestors

At the beggining of the game, choose one enemy Hero per 1000 pts. When it is killed score additional 50 VP and when it kills your Hero, roll a D6. On 4+ place a Royal Spectre of type of the killed character, with equipnament depending on its type, but without Runes.

Dwarfen Determination

Your army may re-roll failed Break and Panic tests when in Command Range of a King.

Grudge to Settle

When enemy captures your banner, all your units get +1 to hit against that unit. When you destroy that unit, your enemy looses your banner.

Dishonoured Engineer Guild Special Rules

Army of Dishonoured

Engineers within this Army gain Immune to Psychology and Loner. Also they can join Loner Unbreakable units and then they gain Unbreakable rule as well. Loner Character can and must be your General.

Forbidden Armoury

Thanes and Engineers in this Army may buy Experimental Shotgun(look Pirate Slayers) ranged weapon for 7pts each. Warriors and Slayers may buy Brace of Pistols for 1pts each.

Tatoos and Anger everywhere

All Loner units in this army improve their Movement and Magic Resistance by 1. If any Unit shall have Magic Resistance(-3), they may also buy 6+ Ward Save for +1pts per model.

Experimental

Each time your unit rolls a 1 on a ranged attack(hit, wound or Multiple Shoots), roll a D6(with re-roll permitted if any Engineer is within 6" of this Unit). On 1 or 2 that unit looses 1 Wound(with only Ward Save possible). However your entire army gains 5+ Ward Save against Wounds caused by themselves and Armour Bane(1) on all Ranged Weapons.

New Dwarven Runes


Hull Runes

These Hull Runes may be enscribed on any type of Dwarven Aircraft. Points cap of those runes depends on type of that Aircraft.
  • Battle Barge - 100pts
  • Gyrobomber - 50pts
  • Gyrocopters or Scout Gyrocopters - 30pts
In units such as Gyrocopters, it counts as single magic item, affecting all models in this unit.



Master Rune of Bombardment - 40pts

Battle Barge or Gyrobomber with this rune gains +1 modifier to Wound roll when using Bombarding Cataclysm or Bombing Run special rule.


Rune of Propulsion - 15pts

This unit gains +1 to its Fly special rule(stackable to max 10).


Rune of Aerial Precision - 35pts

This unit gains +1 to its Bs characteristic.


Master Rune of Honour - 30pts

It is a Hull Rune, but may also be given for an Engineer. On an Aircraft: Single model in this unit may become a Battle Standard Bearer for +15pts.

On an Engineer: This Engineer gains an option to mount an Aircraft. Points of this Aircraft are added to cost of this Engineer. It counts as riding a Behemoth, while using Split Profile(Chariot). This Aircraft's Hull runes do not count to Engineer's magic item points cap(including this rune), but Engineer looses also 1 Wound(2 if riding Battle Barge).


Rune of Plating - 20pts

This unit gains Armoured Hide(1) special rule against shooting(Stackable up to 2).


Rune of Arrival - 10pts

This unit may re-roll its first Charge roll in the game.


Master Rune of Air Ambush - 25pts

This unit may be placed in Ambush, but when placing it, you may set it on map as if it was Scout before start of game. It will be unable to move this turn if it used this special type of deployment.
